# Synamon Personalities (Locked — 2026-04-12)

Personalities feed into: tamagotchi needs-decay rate, preferred food, idle quirks, reaction animations, and battle AI.

Legendaries (Omenix, Lunveil) are intentionally aloof — keeping them is like keeping a pet dragon, not a pet cat.

| Brezzet | Curious, easily distracted | Attention meter drains fast but refills easily, likes novelty |
