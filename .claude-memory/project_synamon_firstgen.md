# Synamon — First Generation (Locked)

## Region: The Luminal Reaches
High-altitude plateau ringed by ancient coral-limestone cliffs. A long-dried inland sea left salt flats, bioluminescent mineral veins, and fossilized reef formations. Bisected by the **Resonant Rift** canyon (crystal pillars, perpetual harmonic vibrations). Heart: **Aureum Basin** (geothermal hot spring lake, perpetually misted). Nights cold and clear; mineral veins glow at dusk.

Ecological zones: salt flats, fossilized coral, Resonant Rift canyon, Aureum Basin hot springs, crystal mineral formations, deep canyon shadows/cave networks, high-altitude starlight.
