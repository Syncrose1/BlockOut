# Synamon Type System (Locked — revised 2026-04-12)

## 14 Types

**Primary-eligible (elemental/core):** Ignis, Aqua, Terra, Ventus, Umbra, Lux, Sonus, Arcanus, Spiritus, Normal

**Secondary-only (trait types):** Flying, Ferrous, Venom, Natura

Flying and Ferrous are secondary-only by design — no pure Flying or pure Ferrous primary species except where a species has Normal as its "real" primary.

---

## Arcanus / Spiritus Split (was all Arcanus)

**Arcanus** = learned/constructed magic. Runes, sigils, spells, arcane force. Active and wilful.
**Spiritus** = innate/transcendent being. Soul-stuff, ethereal matter, cosmic presence. Passive and ancient.

| Species | Old Type | New Type | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Runekit | Arcanus | **Arcanus** | Sigil/rune magic on fur — textbook arcane caster |
| Fluxling | Arcanus/Ignis | **Arcanus**/Ignis | Unstable arcane energy, not spiritual |
| Spectrix | Arcanus/Lux | **Spiritus**/Lux | Crystal-glass deer, translucent, soul-like |
| Glassling | Arcanus/Flying | **Spiritus**/Flying | Ethereal jellyfish — drifting spirit creature |
| Omenix | Arcanus/Umbra | **Spiritus**/Umbra | Embodies the moon's face — prophetic, liminal |
| Lunveil | Arcanus/Umbra | **Spiritus**/Umbra | Dark side of moon, legendary — cosmic/spiritual |

---

## Chuffin — Normal/Flying

Chuffin gains **Normal** as primary type. Flying was always its secondary.
Normal identity: no resistances, no weaknesses, broad move access.
Fits Chuffin's charismatic-but-ordinary puffin personality.

---

## Venom Secondary — Darkspore, Windmite, Cloakrit (locked)

Three species reassigned to Venom secondary. All updated in species.json.

| Species | Old Secondary | New Secondary | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Darkspore | Natura | **Venom** | Shadow-spores are toxic by nature |
| Windmite | Terra | **Venom** | Pill bug arthropod — rolling venom delivery fits |
| Cloakrit | Aqua | **Venom** | Light-absorbing ambush crab — predatory venom fits better than Aqua |
