BST values are base values at Lv1 of each stage. Stats grow with level within the stage. Each evolution gates the next +50 BST tier. Legendaries are single-stage — BST fixed at 500, no evolution. Max 1 legendary per team.

Level Scaling (within stage)

stat(lv) = baseStat × (1 + (lv − 1) × GROWTH_RATE)
HP(lv) = baseHP × (1 + (lv − 1) × GROWTH_RATE) + lv × 5

GROWTH_RATE ~0.02 (2% per level). Same rate for all species — proportional balance is preserved. Exact value tuned during implementation.

Speed Model

attacksThisTurn = floor(yourSPD / enemySPD)  [min 1]

SPD has two meaningful thresholds only:

yourSPD > enemySPDYou act first — 1 attack each
yourSPD ≥ 2× enemySPDYou get 2 attacks before they act
yourSPD ≥ 3× enemySPD3 attacks before they act — and so on

Level scaling preserves the ratio — floor(A/B) stays roughly constant as both stats grow, so constitution SPD advantages hold through levelling.

Damage Formula

damage = (atk / def) × power × typeMulti × rand(0.85–1.0)

Constitutions
